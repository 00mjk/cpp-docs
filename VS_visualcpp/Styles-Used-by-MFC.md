# Styles Used by MFC
Use the following styles when you create the corresponding MFC object. In most cases, these styles are set in the `dwStyle` parameter of the class **Create** function.  
  
### MFC Styles  
  
|Style|Description|  
|-----------|-----------------|  
|[Button styles](../VS_visualcpp/Button-Styles.md)|Applies to [CButton Class](../VS_visualcpp/CButton-Class.md) objects, such as radio buttons, check boxes and pushbuttons. Specify a combination of styles in the `dwStyle` parameter of [CButton::Create](../Topic/CButton::Create.md).|  
|[Combo-box styles](../VS_visualcpp/Combo-Box-Styles.md)|Applies to [CComboBox Class](../VS_visualcpp/CComboBox-Class.md) objects. Specify a combination of styles in the `dwStyle` parameter of [CComboBox::Create](../Topic/CComboBox::Create.md).|  
|[Edit styles](../VS_visualcpp/Edit-Styles.md)|Applies to [CEdit Class](../VS_visualcpp/CEdit-Class.md) objects. Specify a combination of styles in the `dwStyle` parameter of [CEdit::Create](../Topic/CEdit::Create.md).|  
|[Frame-window styles](../VS_visualcpp/Frame-Window-Styles--MFC-.md)|Applies to [CFrameWnd Class](../VS_visualcpp/CFrameWnd-Class.md) objects. Specify a combination of styles in the `dwStyle` parameter of [CFrameWnd::Create](../Topic/CFrameWnd::Create.md).|  
|[List-box styles](../VS_visualcpp/List-Box-Styles.md)|Applies to [CListBox Class](../VS_visualcpp/CListBox-Class.md) objects. Specify a combination of styles in the `dwStyle` parameter of [CListBox::Create](../Topic/CListBox::Create.md).|  
|[Message-box styles](../VS_visualcpp/Message-Box-Styles.md)|Applies to [AfxMessageBox](../Topic/AfxMessageBox.md) items. Specify a combination of styles in the `nType` parameter of `AfxMessageBox`.|  
|[Scroll-bar styles](../VS_visualcpp/Scroll-Bar-Styles.md)|Applies to [CScrollBar Class](../VS_visualcpp/CScrollBar-Class.md) objects. Specify a combination of styles in the `dwStyle` parameter of [CScrollBar::Create](../Topic/CScrollBar::Create.md).|  
|[Static styles](../VS_visualcpp/Static-Styles.md)|Applies to [CStatic Class](../VS_visualcpp/CStatic-Class.md) objects. Specify a combination of styles in the `dwStyle` parameter of [CStatic::Create](../Topic/CStatic::Create.md).|  
|[Window styles](../VS_visualcpp/Window-Styles.md)|Applies to [CWnd Class](../VS_visualcpp/CWnd-Class.md) objects. Specify a combination of styles in the `dwStyle` parameter of [CWnd::Create](../Topic/CWnd::Create.md) or [CWnd::CreateEx](../Topic/CWnd::CreateEx.md).|  
|[Extended window styles](../VS_visualcpp/Extended-Window-Styles.md)|Applies to [CWnd Class](../VS_visualcpp/CWnd-Class.md) objects. Specify a combination of styles in the `dwExStyle` parameter of [CWnd::CreateEx](../Topic/CWnd::CreateEx.md).|
