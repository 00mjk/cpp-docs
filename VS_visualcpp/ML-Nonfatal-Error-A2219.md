# ML Nonfatal Error A2219
**Bad alignment for offset in unwind code**  
  
 The operand for [.ALLOCSTACK](../VS_visualcpp/.ALLOCSTACK.md) and [.SAVEREG](../VS_visualcpp/.SAVEREG.md) must be a multiple of 8.  The operand for [.SAVEXMM128](../VS_visualcpp/.SAVEXMM128.md) and [.SETFRAME](../VS_visualcpp/.SETFRAME.md) must be a multiple of 16.
