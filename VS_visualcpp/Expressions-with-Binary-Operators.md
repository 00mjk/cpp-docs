# Expressions with Binary Operators
Binary operators act on two operands in an expression. The binary operators are:  
  
-   [Multiplicative operators](../VS_visualcpp/Multiplicative-Operators-and-the-Modulus-Operator.md)  
  
    -   Multiplication (*)  
  
    -   Division (/)  
  
    -   Modulus (%)  
  
-   [Additive operators](../VS_visualcpp/Additive-Operators----and--.md)  
  
    -   Addition (+)  
  
    -   Subtraction (–)  
  
-   [Shift operators](../VS_visualcpp/Left-Shift-and-Right-Shift-Operators-----and----.md)  
  
    -   Right shift (>>)  
  
    -   Left shift (<<)  
  
-   [Relational and equality operators](../VS_visualcpp/Relational-Operators---------=--and--=.md)  
  
    -   Less than (<)  
  
    -   Greater than (>)  
  
    -   Less than or equal to (<=)  
  
    -   Greater than or equal to (>=)  
  
    -   Equal to (==)  
  
    -   Not equal to (!=)  
  
-   Bitwise operators  
  
    -   [Bitwise AND (&)](../VS_visualcpp/Bitwise-AND-Operator---.md)  
  
    -   [Bitwise exclusive OR (^)](../VS_visualcpp/Bitwise-Exclusive-OR-Operator--^.md)  
  
    -   [Bitwise inclusive OR (&#124;)](../VS_visualcpp/Bitwise-Inclusive-OR-Operator---.md)  
  
-   Logical operators  
  
    -   [Logical AND (&&)](../VS_visualcpp/Logical-AND-Operator----.md)  
  
    -   [Logical OR (&#124;&#124;)](../VS_visualcpp/Logical-OR-Operator----.md)  
  
-   [Assignment operators](../VS_visualcpp/Assignment-Operators.md)  
  
    -   Assignment (=)  
  
    -   Addition assignment (+=)  
  
    -   Subtraction assignment (–=)  
  
    -   Multiplication assignment (*=)  
  
    -   Division assignment (/=)  
  
    -   Modulus assignment (%=)  
  
    -   Left shift assignment (<<=)  
  
    -   Right shift assignment (>>=)  
  
    -   Bitwise AND assignment (&=)  
  
    -   Bitwise exclusive OR assignment (^=)  
  
    -   Bitwise inclusive OR assignment (&#124;=)  
  
-   [Comma Operator](../VS_visualcpp/Comma-Operator---.md) (,)
