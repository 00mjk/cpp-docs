# Linker Tools Warning LNK4002
symbol defined in object  
  
 The symbol, displayed in its decorated form, was specified in its undecorated form in `object`, but a unique match to a decorated symbol could not be found. This warning is always preceded by warning [LNK4022](../VS_visualcpp/Linker-Tools-Warning-LNK4022.md) and followed by fatal error [LNK1152](../VS_visualcpp/Linker-Tools-Error-LNK1152.md).
